RSVP

Guests

What is the venue's address?

The Cloisters 10440 Falls Road Lutherville, MD 21093

Is the ceremony and reception in two different places?

No, the ceremony and reception will be held at the same venue.

What should I wear?

You should wear formal, black-tie, or cocktail attire.

Do you have a registry?

We do not have a registry, being able to celebrate with you is a gift in itself! We will still provide a table guests can place gifts, any gifts containing checks or cash will be used towards down-payment for a house.

Is there a bar?

We do not have a bar, however there will be a champagne toast during the event.

When do I have to RSVP by?

Please RSVP by August 17, 2024. This is so we can get an appropriate headcount for catering.

I am unable to attend in person, will the ceremony be livestreamed?

Yes, we have not finalized these details but the ceremony will be livestreamed. Please check back later for more information.

How can I request a song for the reception?

On your invitation there should be a card to RSVP by mail. You can either request the song(s) there or through the electronic RSVP form.

Wedding Party

Everyone - I'm out of town, do I have to be there prior to the wedding?

You are under no obligation whatsoever to be there for any day other than the day of the wedding. You don't need to be there for the rehearsal or help with any set-up. Knowing this, if you would rather come as a guest instead (or not come to the wedding at all), we understand and we hope to celebrate with you another time.

Bridesmaids - What color should my dress be?

Dusty Lavender is the preferred dress color. It seems like most of the chiffon dresses come in this color. If a dress is not available in this color, then please reach out to us. Once you have picked, let us know the style number of the dress so we can tell the boutique and order them all at once.

Bridesmaids - What shoes and/or accessories should I wear?

You should wear comfortable flats in a neutral color (matching your skin tone) or a tone similar to the dress. If you would like to wear other accessories, then simple gold-colored jewelry would be perfect, but you do not have to wear jewelry.

Groomsmen - What kind of suit should I wear?

A suit, two or three piece in a dark or medium grey is preferred. The wedding venue does not have AC so you should get a lighter suit weight if possible. We are hoping that since the wedding is in October the weather will be nice. For the ties, we are going to provide them closer to the wedding date.
